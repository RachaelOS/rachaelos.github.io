Showcasing my hobbies

There is a page dedicated to each of my hobbies to showcase my development and creations.

I began photography in summer 2022 when I bought my secondhand Sony NEX-6 mirrorless camera and 16-50mm lens. My main interest is in wildlife and landscape photography.
