Introduction

Hi, I'm Rachael and I am solo-developing the game MoonBase using Godot! MoonBase is a 2D RPG game where you help to build a base on the Moon through exploring and crafting. You complete missions assigned from military, science or commercial industries to unlock new items and grow your skills. Also look out for the rogue robots causing mayhem!
