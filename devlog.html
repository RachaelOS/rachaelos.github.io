For updates and info on MoonBase development

MoonBase is an cosy base-buidling game set on the Moon. Explore the lunar surface, uncover ancient secrets, and battle alien creatures in this sci-fi adventure! Your job is to build and maintain the base and help humanity thrive on the Moon. Complete tasks to develop your skills and unlock exciting abilities and tools!
